A Marketplace for Carpenters

TheWoodWorks is the global marketplace for unique, handmade, carpentry goods. It houses a variety of handcrafted wooden goods, of everything from furniture, to tables, dressers, chairs, cutting boards, boxes, bird houses, pots, carvings, and more.

We know how hard it can be for carpenters to start and maintain a small business. That is why we built a place where carpenters can list and sell their products to a targeted audience, and connect with other carpenters around the world. We help our community or carpenters turn their hobbies into successful businesses. Our platform connects them with millions of buyers looking for unique wooden pieces to display in their homes.

How TheWoodWorks Works

Our global marketplace is a vibrant community of real people connecting over special wooden goods. The platform empowers sellers to do what they love while earning an income, and helps buyers find the perfect item to fit their wants and needs.

Sell Easily

With low fees, powerful tools, and expert support, we help carpenters start, manage, and scale their businesses. Want to make money with your skills? Become a seller today!

Buy Effortlessly

From specific to custom made, our search tools help buyers explore all the special one-of-a-kind items offered by TheWoodWorks sellers. Our marketplace homepage is curated with current trends and great deals.

Shop Securely

We provide the technology behind all sales on TheWoodWorks, helping buyers and sellers connect and exchange with no hassle. Keeping those connections safe, fun, and secure is our priority and our customer service is always here to help.
