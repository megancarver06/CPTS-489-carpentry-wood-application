Meet the Artist

Johnny Appleseed

Johnny Appleseed, the skilled carpenter behind Timber Treasures, has been crafting exquisite handmade furniture since 2008. With a keen eye for design and a dedication to quality, Johnny has established Timber Treasures as a premier destination for modern bedroom and office furniture. Each piece emanates a sense of artistry and functionality, reflecting Johnny's passion for his craft and commitment to providing customers with timeless pieces that enhance any space.

At Timber Treasures, Johnny Appleseed's craftsmanship goes beyond mere functionality, infusing each piece with a unique blend of style and durability. With a meticulous attention to detail, Johnny sources only the finest materials to ensure that every creation meets his exacting standards. Whether it's a sleek, minimalist desk for a contemporary office or a cozy, inviting bed for a modern bedroom, each item from Timber Treasures is a testament to Johnny's expertise and commitment to excellence.

Beyond crafting furniture, Johnny Appleseed's vision extends to creating environments that inspire and elevate daily living. By seamlessly blending form and function, Johnny's designs strike the perfect balance between aesthetics and practicality. Whether clients seek to transform their living spaces into tranquil retreats or productive work environments, Timber Treasures offers an array of meticulously crafted pieces that embody Johnny's signature style and dedication to superior craftsmanship.

Outside of his woodworking pursuits, Johnny Appleseed is an avid nature enthusiast, often spending his free time hiking through the nearby forests and sketching the beauty of the wilderness. Despite his busy schedule, he prioritizes quality time with his family, sharing his love for woodworking with his children and instilling in them the values of creativity and craftsmanship. With a deep appreciation for both the natural world and the warmth of family bonds, Johnny embodies a holistic approach to life that enriches both his craft and his relationships.
